Vela Getting Started Guide

Welcome to Vela
Vela is a project management tool built for teams who need clarity without complexity. This guide covers everything you need to get your workspace set up and your team moving.

Creating your workspace
When you first sign up, you will be prompted to create a workspace. Your workspace is the top-level container for all your projects, members, and settings. Choose a name that reflects your team or organization. You can rename your workspace at any time from Settings > Workspace.

Inviting team members
Go to Settings > Members > Invite Members and enter the email addresses of the people you want to add. They will receive an invitation email with a link to join. Members can be assigned one of three roles:
- Owner: full administrative access, manages billing and workspace settings
- Admin: can create and delete projects, manage members, but cannot access billing
- Member: can create and edit tasks within projects they are assigned to

Creating your first project
Click "New Project" from the dashboard or sidebar. Enter a project name and optional description. You can choose a list view, board view (Kanban), or timeline view as your default. Projects can be made private (visible only to assigned members) or open (visible to all workspace members).

Adding and managing tasks
Within a project, click "Add Task" or press N to create a new task. Each task can have:
- Title and description
- Assignee (one or more workspace members)
- Due date
- Priority (low, medium, high, urgent)
- Custom fields (available on Pro and Business plans)
- Attachments and comments

Organizing with sections
Tasks can be grouped into sections within a project. Sections function like swimlanes in board view or collapsible groups in list view. To add a section, click "Add Section" at the bottom of your task list.

Using the inbox
The inbox in the left sidebar collects all notifications: task assignments, comments mentioning you (@username), due date reminders, and status changes on tasks you follow. Mark items as read or archive them to keep your inbox clean.

Exporting project data
Navigate to your project, click the three-dot menu in the top right, and select Export. Choose CSV for a spreadsheet-compatible format or PDF for a formatted report. Note: export is available on Starter and above for CSV; PDF export requires Pro or higher.

To access your API key, go to Settings > Developer > API Keys. API keys are scoped per workspace and can be rotated at any time. Each key can be restricted to read-only or full access.

Keyboard shortcuts
- N: new task
- Cmd/Ctrl + K: command palette (search anything)
- Cmd/Ctrl + P: print / export current view
- G then D: go to dashboard
- G then I: go to inbox
- ?: show all shortcuts

Getting help
Vela support can be reached via the in-app help widget (click the ? icon in the bottom right), email at support@vela.app, or by submitting a ticket at help.vela.app. Pro customers receive a 24-hour response SLA. Business customers receive a 4-hour response SLA.
